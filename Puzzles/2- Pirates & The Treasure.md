# My approach <br>
I solve it backwards. I figure out what each pirate would get if the current leader were thrown overboard — that's their 'walk-away' value. 
The leader then bribes the cheapest pirates to vote yes by giving them just one coin more than their walk-away, and buys only as many votes as he needs to hit 50%.
Doing this from 2 pirates up to 5, the leader ends up giving 1 coin each to the two pirates who'd otherwise get nothing (P3 and P1) and keeps 98 for himself
