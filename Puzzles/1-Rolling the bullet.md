# My approach
The two bullets sit side by side, an empty chamber is almost always followed by another empty one — so not spinning keeps you on that safe empty run (only 1 of the 4 empties leads into a bullet, 25%), whereas spinning destroys that pattern and drops you back to the plain 2-out-of-6 odds (33%) — so shuffling raises the chance of firing.
