# Reading Roadmap — Kais Khan

Two parallel tracks: Discretionary Trading (DT) and Quant.
DT books read in DT block (7–9 PM). Quant books read in Quant blocks when their month arrives.
Rule: One book at a time. Finish before starting the next.

---

## 📈 DT BOOK SEQUENCE (in order)

| # | Book | Author | Focus |
|---|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Trading in the Zone | Mark Douglas | Psychology / discipline |
| 2 | The Disciplined Trader | Mark Douglas | Psychology depth |
| 3 | Master the Markets | Tom Williams | VSA — volume spread analysis |
| 4 | Mind Over Markets | James Dalton | Market Profile |
| 5 | Trade Your Way to Financial Freedom | Van Tharp | System design + expectancy |
| 6 | Definitive Guide to Position Sizing | Van Tharp | Position sizing |
| 7 | The Mathematics of Money Management | Ralph Vince | Money management math (quant bridge) |

Flow: mindset → method → math
Books 5–7 connect directly to Quant Month 6 (Risk Management).
